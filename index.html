About Us

Welcome to Warm Bliss Hotel, your home away from home! Located at Lekki, Nigeria. Our hotel blends elegance, comfort, and exceptional service to create a memorable stay for every guest. We take pride in offering Luxurious Accommodations of modern settings with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a work desk and free Wi-Fi access all with a private bathroom. You can also enjoy our recreational facilites such as our swimming pool, fitness center, and spa treatments during your leisure time.

Warm Bliss hotel is located one mile from the Lekki Conservation Centre, a peaceful nature reserve offering a canopy walkway, wildlife, and a break from the hustle and bustle of the city

So whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, our dedicated team is here to provide personalized hospitality that meets your every need. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be immersed in an atmosphere of warmth and refinement.
